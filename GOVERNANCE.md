# Governance

## Project Goal

Maintain a high-quality, community-driven reference list of Android apps that
support App Functions.

## Roles

- Maintainers: review contributions, enforce standards, manage roadmap.
- Contributors: propose and improve entries with verifiable evidence.

## Decision Model

- Normal changes: consensus among maintainers.
- Tie-breaker: lead maintainer decision.
- Urgent moderation/security actions: any maintainer may act immediately.

## Contribution Principles

- Verifiability over hype.
- Neutral and factual tone.
- Clear provenance of sources.
- No spam, no duplicates.

## Inactive Maintainers

Maintainers inactive for 90+ days may be rotated out to keep reviews timely.
